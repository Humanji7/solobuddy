### Hook Formulas (2025)

#### 1. Curiosity Gap
**–ú–µ—Ö–∞–Ω–∏–∫–∞:** Present incomplete information ‚Üí psychological tension ‚Üí must read to satisfy curiosity

**–°—Ç—Ä—É–∫—Ç—É—Ä–∞:** Leave knowledge gap, hint at unknown, readers feel compelled to fill gap

**–ü—Ä–∏–º–µ—Ä:**
- "I doubled my income ‚Äî and still felt broke."
- "My content was going viral. My DMs were dead quiet."

---

#### 2. Contrarian Hook
**–ú–µ—Ö–∞–Ω–∏–∫–∞:** Challenge widely-held belief ‚Üí creates tension + curiosity

**–°—Ç—Ä—É–∫—Ç—É—Ä–∞:** "[Common belief] is wrong. Here's what actually works:"

---

#### 3. Transformation Hook
**–ú–µ—Ö–∞–Ω–∏–∫–∞:** Before-and-after scenario readers relate to

**–°—Ç—Ä—É–∫—Ç—É—Ä–∞:** "I went from [bad situation] to [good outcome] in [timeframe]. Here's exactly how:"

---

#### 4. Specific Number Hook
**–ú–µ—Ö–∞–Ω–∏–∫–∞:** Odd numbers and specific figures outperform round numbers

**–°—Ç—Ä—É–∫—Ç—É—Ä–∞:** "[Specific number] [lessons/mistakes/strategies] from [experience/timeframe]:"

**–ü—Ä–∏–º–µ—Ä:** "17 mistakes from 3 years building in public" –≤–º–µ—Å—Ç–æ "20 lessons from 5 years"

**–°—Ç—Ä—É–∫—Ç—É—Ä–∞:** "If you're a [specific audience], read this:"

### Authenticity Over Performance

> **Best filter:** If sharing feels like work or performance, don't share it. If it feels like genuine value to other founders, do.

> **Critical:** The moment you optimize vulnerability for engagement, you stop being vulnerable.

### Sharing Failures

> "Failing in startup happens, and sharing failures is something most people 'building in public' won't do."

> **Real BIP:** If someone only shares good news, they're not really building in public ‚Äî they're running a marketing campaign.

---

### Transparency Guidelines

**DO:**
- Respect counterparties ‚Äî keep private matters involving third parties private
- Your transparency shouldn't damage other people's reputations or relationships

**DON'T:**
- Early: Share raw metrics to build credibility
- Later: Same openness may reveal too much to competitors

**Balance shifts as product gains traction.**
