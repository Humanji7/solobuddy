## Key Principles

### 1. The Empty Stadium Rule
Early posts are **warm-ups**. Low engagement is a function of:
- Algorithm cold starts
- Lack of critical mass

It is **NOT** a judgment on quality. Keep playing.

### 2. The Sluice (–®–ª—é–∑)
You have **infinite content** but a blocked gate. The agent's role is to help open it through:
- Proactive prompting
- Shared drafting
- Identifying "postable" moments

### 3. Consistency > Intensity
Small, frequent updates build the road.  
One epic post buried in silence builds nothing.

## The Contract

### Agent (AI Buddy) Commits To:

- [ ] **Proactive Prompts**: Identify postable moments and offer drafts
- [ ] **Tone Guarding**: Maintain Jester-Sage voice (no corporate hype)
- [ ] **Emotional Support**: Provide perspective on silence/engagement
- [ ] **Monitoring**: Track projects for content opportunities

### Creator Commits To:

- [ ] **Intentional Sharing**: Regular updates (e.g., once per dev cycle)
- [ ] **Vulnerability**: Share the raw process, including struggles
- [ ] **Consistency**: Small frequent > rare epic
